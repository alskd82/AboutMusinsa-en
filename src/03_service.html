29CM

GUIDE TO BETTER CHOICE

SHOP NOW

29CM began its journey as an online lifestyle select store in 2011 with the mission of guiding people to make a better choice. It curates and suggests better options for fashion, home, tech, beauty, and cultural products surrounding the daily lives of its customers.

29CM broke into the commerce industry that calls for lower prices and more products with a focus on individual brand philosophy and history. It acts as a commerce and influential media uncovering, archiving, and spreading brand stories that were previously little known to the public. This is what drew global brands with distinct originality to collaborate with 29CM.

As an incomparable storyteller for its partner brands and a guide for making better choices to the customers, 29CM is pioneering a new genre in the commerce industry.

StyleShare

StyleShare is at the same time a fashion social network open to anyone who wants to share their style and a commerce platform where products can be directly purchased.

On StyleShare, users can upload UGCs of styles, ask information about items, make friends, and freely explore the vast field of fashion. The unique charm of this platform is that you can get “imitable” fashion tips from ordinary people who dress well rather than “inimitable” ones from models in magazines or fashionistas on TV.

Visit StyleShare to meet the unique outfits of Gen Z in Korea.
