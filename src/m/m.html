History

MUSINSA started as a sneaker community in 2001 and has been expanding into a distribution and marketing platform for fashion brands and designers.

We deliver an engaging customer experience to men, women, and children with a vast range of curated fashion brands and styles in casual, formal, contemporary, luxury, and sportswear categories.
