OUR STORY

2022

lazy night,
an online select shop for Gen-X fashion and lifestyle

lazy night is an online select shop for female Gen X customers who are keen on stylish and trendy fashion. The shop showcases curated offering that encompasses fashion, tech, home, beauty, and cultural products, reflecting the lifestyle of female Gen X who are well-versed in online shopping.

2022

MUSINSA Player,
an online select shop for athletic sports apparel and equipment

MUSINSA Player is an athletic sports platform that offers sports apparel and equipment to avid players of a wide range of indoor and outdoor sports.

2022

MUSINSA Kids,
an online select shop for kids fashion and lifestyle

MUSINSA Kids is a store for young parents looking to style their children with trendy fashion brands. MUSINSA Kids collaborates with mainstream fashion brands to launch the Kids collection and introduces family looks with matching outfits for parents and children.

2021

Acquisition of
‍29CM and StyleShare

MUSINSA acquired 29CM and StyleShare in an initiative to expand customer demography and product offering. 29CM is the fastest growing online fashion and lifestyle select shop focusing on 25 – 39s female, and StyleShare is a community-led fashion and beauty e-commerce for Gen Z.

2021

MUSINSA Golf,
an online select shop for golf

MUSINSA Golf offers hip and trendy golf apparel and equipment, which can complement both golf outings and casual occasions.

2021

MUSINSA Boutique,
an online select shop for luxury fashion and lifestyle

MUSINSA Boutique is an online select shop dedicated to luxury fashion and lifestyle products which are sourced from official partnerships with global luxury brands.

2020

soldout,
a re-commerce platform

soldout, a re-commerce platform embodying MUSINSA’s expertise and know-how of fashion, trades limited editions of clothing, fashion accessory, lifestyle, luxury goods, etc.

2020

MUSINSA Beauty,
an online select shop for beauty

MUSINSA Beauty offers a wide range of beauty products as well as contents including product ranking, recommendations, styling tips and magazine contents.

2017

MUSINSA Standard,
a private brand of MUSINSA

MUSINSA Standard offers the best price for quality products. It showcases basic fashion line, especially renowned for high quality slacks and blazer jackets.

2009

MUSINSA STORE,
an online fashion and lifestyle store

MUSINSA STORE established itself as an online select shop for authentic and exclusive designer brands. It offers products with a trend-setting design that are absent in brick-and-mortar stores.

2005

MUSINSA Magazine,
a fashion magazine

MUSINSA Magazine quickly emerged as a leading media for fashion news including designer and creator interviews, on-the-scene reporting of brand showcases, product releases, etc. It is a channel for MUSINSA original contents such as brand pictorials, street fashion looks, and styling suggestions.

2003

MUSINSA.com,
an online fashion community

MUSINSA.com scaled to be a go-to online fashion portal beyond sneakers, and soon became the mecca of street fashion and culture.

2001

MUSINSA,
‍an online sneaker community

MUSINSA started as an online community for sneakerheads, where the community members share opinions and information on limited editions, and reproduce the sneaker culture.
