OUR STORY

2022

MUSINSA Player, which showcases a broad range of sports brands is opened

MUSINSA Player is a sports platform that trades any and all products related to indoor sports like ball games, as well as to outdoor sports like hiking and camping. It offers various contents and promotional events for customers who love and truly enjoy sports.

2022

MUSINSA Kids, a chic online fashion and lifestyle store for kids is opened

MUSINSA Kids is a store that caters to the needs of young parents looking to style their children with brand fashion. MUSINSA Kids introduces the Kids lines of popular brands and suggests family looks with matching outfits for parents and children.

2021

Pursued the M&A of 29CM and StyleShare

2021

MUSINSA Golf, which offers a wide selection of golf fashion is opened

MUSINSA Golf, which targets young golfers in their 20s and 30s, is a store committed to introducing differentiated and stylish fashion brands and products . It offers varied fashion styles, not only for longtime golfers but also for beginner golfers.

2021

MUSINSA Beauty, a stylish online beauty store is launched

MUSINSA Boutique, a luxury select store that officially partners with various global brands, introduces products from these labels to consumers in Korea. It collaborates with these brands on online sales and marketing to help their businesses succeed in Korea.

2020

soldout, a resale platform for limited editions is launched

Soldout is a resale platform that embodies the expertise and know-how of MUSINSA, which started as an online community for shoe maniacs. Although soldout’s beginnings were humble and limited to sneakers, it now trades massive collections of limited editions that include clothing, fashion accessories, lifestyle and tech products, etc.

2020

MUSINSA Beauty, a stylish online beauty store is launched

MUSINSA Beauty makes professional beauty trend recommendations based on MUSINSA’s accumulated know-how. It provides a wide range of information on beauty products, such as their rankings and magazine contents, and suggests styles that match the makeup.

2017

MUSINSA Standard, which offers essential casual clothing for men, women, and children is launched

MUSINSA Standard, an in-house brand of MUSINSA, sells quality products at reasonable prices. It showcases basic items of various fits and colors, among which slacks and blazer jackets have been gaining much popularity.

2009

MUSINSA Store, an online fashion store is launched

MUSINSA Store established itself as an online select store specializing in the sale of authentic designer and streetwear brands. It offers products with a one-of-a-kind mood and design that are hard to find in department stores and serves as a medium for promoting countless brands.

2005

MUSINSA Magazine, which provides insights on the latest fashion trends and brand stories is published

MUSINSA Magazine quickly emerged as a representative channel for communicating various fashion news—designer interviews, on-the-scene reporting of brand showcases, product releases, etc. It still maintains the interest of fashion lovers as a channel that delivers essential contents such as brand pictorials, street fashion looks, and coordination and styling suggestions.

2003

MUSINSA.com, a fashion community portal is launched

As the number of community members increased, MUSINSA turned into an online fashion portal where members can enjoy everything about fashion, not just shoes. At the time, street snapshots introducing people with remarkable fashion styles right from the streets received much attention as one of the representative contents of MUSINSA.com.

2001

MUSINSA, an online community for sneaker fans is started

MUSINSA started as an online community for shoe lovers. Our members shared and enjoyed the sneaker culture, exchanging photos of and information about limited edition shoes of labels.
